Sunset Sky Compilation 2021. This is a project from Art 74 where we took images and glicthed them using Audacity. I used an image that I took near my house of the sky which I really liked beacsue of the colors that showed up. I really enjoy taking pictures of random things and sunsets on my free time. I like capturing moments when I can and images like these remind me of those special moments. The original image is also placed at the top left corner of the compilation. I like how the images came out since there was a variety of glitches based on the way I experimented with the effects on Audacity. There was also a lot of greens and purples that showed up after the effects which was pretty cool to see.
