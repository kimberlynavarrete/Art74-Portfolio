Biography

I am a 19 year old pictorial artist and student living in San Jose, California studying at San Jose State University. I am finishing my second year going onto my third year at SJSU under the BFA Pictorial Art major. Being a full time student has allowed me to take courses that have definitely expanded my knowledge in this field. My experience with materials and techniques was very limited before college but now I have greater opportunities to learn and create. My work consists of paintings, drawings, and occasionally some 3D projects. I love creating work that allows me to continue to learn new techniques and expand my knowledge on materials. I am constantly looking for ways to grow, learn, and improve my skills as well as a ways to express my emotions, thoughts, and opinions through my work. I ultimately enjoy looking at the little things in life to gain inspiration and ideas to incorporate into my work.
